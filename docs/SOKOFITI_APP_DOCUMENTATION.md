## **About Us**

### What is Sokofiti?

**Sokofiti** is Kenya's premier mobile classifieds marketplace app that connects buyers and sellers across the country. The name "Sokofiti" is derived from Swahili, embodying the spirit of a vibrant, hidden marketplace where great deals await discovery.

### Our Mission

To provide Kenyans with a safe, simple, and convenient platform to buy and sell anything ‚Äî from everyday items to unique finds ‚Äî right from their smartphones.

### What You Can Do on Sokofiti

- **Buy Products**: Browse thousands of listings across multiple categories, filter by location, price, and more to find exactly what you need
- **Sell Items**: List your products with photos, descriptions, and pricing in just a few taps
- **Chat with Sellers/Buyers**: Communicate directly through our in-app messaging system with text and voice messages
- **Location-Based Search**: Find items near you using GPS-powered location services
- **Job Listings**: Post and apply for job opportunities
- **Verified Sellers**: Look for verified seller badges for added trust and security

## **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)**

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Download the Sokofiti app, tap "Sign Up," and register using your email, phone number, Google account, or Apple ID (iOS only).

**Q: Is Sokofiti free to use?**
A: Yes! Browsing, searching, and chatting with sellers is completely free. Posting basic ads is also free. Premium features and promoted listings may require a subscription.

**Q: What areas does Sokofiti cover?**
A: Sokofiti operates throughout Kenya, with location-based features to help you find items in your area.

### Buying

**Q: How do I contact a seller?**
A: Tap on any listing and use the "Chat" button to send a message directly to the seller.

**Q: How do I know if a seller is trustworthy?**
A: Look for the verified badge on seller profiles. You can also check their ratings and reviews from previous buyers.

**Q: Can I save listings to view later?**
A: Yes! Tap the heart icon on any listing to add it to your favorites.

### Selling

**Q: How do I post an ad?**
A: Tap the "+" button at the bottom of the screen, select a category, add photos and details, set your price, and publish!

**Q: How many photos can I add to my listing?**
A: You can add multiple photos to showcase your item from different angles.

**Q: How long does my ad stay active?**
A: Ads remain active based on your subscription plan. You can renew or edit them anytime from "My Ads."

**Q: Can I edit my ad after posting?**
A: Yes, go to "My Ads" in your profile and tap on the listing you want to edit.

### Payments & Subscriptions

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We support multiple payment options including:
- M-Pesa (via PayStack/Flutterwave)
- Credit/Debit Cards
- Bank Transfer
- PayPal

**Q: What are subscription packages?**
A: Subscription packages allow you to post more ads and access premium features like promoted listings for better visibility.

**Q: How do I upgrade my subscription?**
A: Go to Profile ‚Üí Subscription to view and purchase available packages.

### Account & Safety

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions sent to your email.

**Q: How do I report a suspicious listing or user?**
A: Tap the report button on any listing or profile to flag it for review by our team.

**Q: Can I block a user?**
A: Yes, you can block users from your chat screen or their profile page.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact our support team through the "Contact Us" section in the app settings.

### Technical Issues

**Q: Why can't I see listings in my area?**
A: Make sure location services are enabled for the Sokofiti app. Go to your phone settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Sokofiti ‚Üí Permissions ‚Üí Location.

**Q: The app is not loading properly. What should I do?**
A: Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Close and reopen the app
3. Clear the app cache
4. Update to the latest version
5. Contact support if the issue persists

## **Refund Policy**

### Overview

At Sokofiti, we strive to provide a fair and transparent experience for all our users. This refund policy applies to payments made through the Sokofiti app for subscription packages and premium features.

### Subscription Refunds

**Within 24 Hours of Purchase:**
- If you purchased a subscription package by mistake or experience technical issues preventing you from using the features, you may request a full refund within 24 hours of purchase.

**After 24 Hours:**
- Subscription fees are generally non-refundable after 24 hours as the service/features have been made available to you.
- Partial refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis for unused subscription time due to technical issues on our end.

### When Refunds Are Granted

We will process a refund in the following situations:
1. **Duplicate Payment**: If you were charged twice for the same transaction
2. **Technical Failure**: If the payment was processed but the subscription/features were not activated due to a system error
3. **Unauthorized Transaction**: If you can prove the transaction was made without your authorization
4. **Service Unavailability**: If our services were unavailable for an extended period during your subscription

### When Refunds Are NOT Granted

Refunds will not be issued for:
1. Change of mind after using the service
2. Failure to read the subscription terms before purchase
3. Violation of our Terms of Service leading to account suspension
4. Listings removed due to policy violations
5. Third-party payment processing fees

### How to Request a Refund

1. **In-App Request**: Go to Profile ‚Üí Contact Us ‚Üí Select "Refund Request"
2. **Email**: Send your request to support@sokofiti.ke with:
   - Your registered email/phone number
   - Transaction ID or payment reference
   - Date of transaction
   - Reason for refund request

### Refund Processing Time

- **Review Period**: 3-5 business days
- **Processing Time**: 5-10 business days after approval
- **Payment Method**: Refunds are issued to the original payment method

### Marketplace Transactions

**Important**: Sokofiti is a classifieds platform that connects buyers and sellers. We do not process payments between buyers and sellers for items listed on the platform.

- Transactions between buyers and sellers are conducted independently
- Sokofiti is not responsible for refunds on items purchased from individual sellers
- We encourage users to meet in safe public places and inspect items before payment
- Report any fraudulent sellers immediately through our reporting system

### Contact Us

For refund inquiries or assistance:

- **Phone**: Available in the app under Contact Us
- **In-App**: Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Contact Us
