## ğŸ¨ **NOTIFICATION BEST PRACTICES**

### **Timing Strategy:**
- **Morning (8-10 AM)**: New listings, daily digest
- **Lunch (12-2 PM)**: Price drops, deals
- **Evening (6-8 PM)**: Messages, engagement
- **Weekend**: Special deals, trending items

### **Frequency Limits:**
- **Maximum**: 3 notifications per day
- **Minimum Gap**: 4 hours between notifications
- **User Control**: Allow users to customize notification preferences

### **Personalization:**
- Use user's name when possible
- Reference their location
- Mention specific items they've viewed/saved
- Tailor to their browsing history

### **Call-to-Action:**
- Always include a clear action (View, Reply, Buy, etc.)
- Create urgency without being pushy
- Use emojis strategically (1-2 per notification)
- Keep messages under 100 characters when possible
