## Performance Tips

1. **A/B Testing:** Rotate headlines and descriptions to find best performers
2. **Mobile Optimization:** 80%+ traffic expected from mobile
3. **Location Targeting:** Focus on major Kenyan cities (Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu)
4. **Negative Keywords:** Add competitors' brand names as negative keywords
5. **Conversion Tracking:** Track app downloads and ad posts as conversions
